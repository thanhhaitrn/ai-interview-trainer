Who We Are

Founded by a practicing physician and a senior pharmaceutical executive, Impiricus was created to transform how life sciences companies support physicians. With our unique access to the largest opted-in network of HCPs, their insights, and clinical expertise, we are the leading provider of AI technology and real-time channels that life science companies need to deliver clear, reliable, and evidence-based resources directly into the hands of HCPs. Guided by a council of 2000+ trusted HCP advisors, we ensure every interaction is clinically meaningful, ethically grounded and leads to better patient care.

Job Summary

Impiricus is seeking a Data Analyst Intern to support campaign performance analysis and reporting. This role partners with Client Services and Engineering to analyze data, validate audiences, and deliver clear, actionable insights through reports and visual summaries. Ideal for a detail-oriented student in a quantitative field looking to gain hands-on experience in a fast-paced, collaborative environment.

Duties / Responsibilities

Analyze marketing campaign performance data and help track key metrics.
Support list match validation and audience performance analysis.
Build Excel- or spreadsheet-based reports and visual summaries for internal stakeholders.
Work with Client Services and Engineering teams to support data requests and cross-team projects.
Translate data findings into clear, actionable insights and recommendations.
Help develop repeatable templates and processes for ongoing analytics needs.

Experience

Currently pursuing or recently completed a degree in Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Business Analytics, or a related STEM field.
Strong proficiency in Excel or Google Sheets.
Curious, analytical, and detail-oriented with strong problem-solving skills.
Able to communicate insights through presentations, slides, or written summaries, translating complex data into clear and intuitive takeaways for internal teams and clients.
Able to manage time effectively and work independently in a fast-paced environment.

Nice To Have

Experience with SQL or data visualization tools.
Exposure to marketing, campaign, or performance data.
Interest in or familiarity with the healthcare or biopharma industry.

Compensation

This is a paid internship with an hourly rate of $25, with interns working up to 40 hours per week.

The program runs for 8 weeks and will be offered in two cohorts. Candidates may participate in the cohort that best aligns with their summer schedule.

Cohort 1: June 15, 2026 through August 7, 2026

Cohort 2: July 6, 2026 through August 28, 2026
